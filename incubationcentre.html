The Goh-Bugun Centre

The Goh-Bugun centre is proposed as a space that will allow the community to experiment with craft processes in order to

    • Strengthen existing techniques
    • Understand a diversity of markets starting with the local traditional market,tourism market and the global markets through digital platforms
    • Study materials and processes that will help revive the use of locally available bast fibres

We propose to set up incubation hub to invite collaborators and researchers to work along with the Bugun community in creating a new line of natural fibre based fabric
