Goh-bugun-The Brand Story

The Goh-Bugun brand represents the revival of cultures of crafting that could become an integral part of tribal societies today.

Chom Mosbu, a first time weaver from Bichom village, represents the Goh-Bugun brand story.A young mother, Chom learnt warping and weaving techniques from Momtaz Hagam, a local elder during the workshop. She works at her own pace, from home or with her community of sisters and friends, while her child watches everyone work from his perch on her back.

What is conserved is her home, her parenting, the autonomy over her time and space and the absorptive learning space for the next generation to learn. What is also created is also a livelihood opportunity and this is the meaning of sustainability for us.

The Brand Goh Bugun creates a market identity for this complex and layered process of memory revival and offers Chom and her community an opportunity to not just co-design workshops to strengthen techniques, markets and knowledge of materials but also tell their stories as Bugun in the process.

The Goh Bugun brand label was introduced during the Bugun annual festival of Pham Kho Sowai 2024
