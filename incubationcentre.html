Incubation Centre

The Incubation Centre is a key initiative of the Goh Bugun Project. It serves as a hub for nurturing and promoting traditional crafts, skills, and knowledge of the Goh Bugun tribe. The centre provides resources, training, and support to help community members develop sustainable livelihoods while preserving their cultural heritage.
