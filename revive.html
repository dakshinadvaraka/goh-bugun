Revive

Reviving a fading practice actually means reviving relationships and linkages with latent skill sets, community held repositories of knowledge of techniques and materials. It is also a revival of the value of makers and craftspeople as keepers of memory, history, and identity.

Goh-Bugun workshops to revive loin loom weaving and bamboo and cane craft followed three pedagogical principles.

Our Guiding Principles

  • Revive value of local teachers
  • Stay with known techniques, tools and motifs
  • Let the community set the pace
